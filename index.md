# Quote & Faith

Welcome to Quote & Faith, your daily source of inspiration and encouragement.

## About Quote & Faith

Quote & Faith is a mobile application designed to deliver meaningful quotes and uplifting content to brighten your day. Our app provides:

- **Daily Inspirational Quotes** - Receive carefully curated quotes to motivate and inspire you
- **Faith-Based Content** - Access a collection of spiritual and faith-centered messages
- **Easy Sharing** - Share your favorite quotes with friends and family through TikTok and other platforms
- **Personalized Experience** - Save and organize quotes that resonate with you

## How It Works

Quote & Faith integrates with TikTok to provide a seamless sign-in experience using TikTok Login Kit. This allows you to:

1. Sign in securely with your TikTok account
2. Share inspirational content directly to TikTok
3. Connect with a community of like-minded individuals

## Features

- Browse a curated library of inspirational quotes
- Receive daily notifications with new quotes
- Create personal collections of your favorite quotes
- Share quotes easily to social media
